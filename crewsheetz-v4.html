How it works
Simple by design

CrewSheetz doesn't replace where you post or how you network. It replaces where submissions land — and gives crew a professional presence to share anywhere.

1

Post anywhere. Organize here.

Create a job post, share the link anywhere you recruit. Every applicant fills out one clean form — and it lands in your inbox, not buried in comments.

2

One profile link. Your whole story.

Crew build a profile with their photo, rate, credits, and live availability calendar. Share one link anywhere someone asks if you're available.

3

No more chaos on either side.

Organizers stop losing submissions in DMs. Crew stop typing their resume in every message. Both sides get an organized, professional experience.
